About Us

Net Lab Pro is an educational website developed to help students understand Computer Networks laboratory concepts in a simple and practical manner.

This platform provides lab experiments, explanations, and learning resources commonly used in undergraduate computer science and IT programs.

The website is created and maintained by a computer science student with the goal of supporting academic learning and improving conceptual understanding of networking fundamentals.

Net Lab Pro is intended strictly for educational and learning purposes.

Lab Features

NetLabPro simplifies complex networking concepts by providing:

  • Step-by-step explanations for each lab experiment
  • Detailed screenshots, diagrams, and pdfs for clarity
  • Hands-on examples for DHCP, DNS, HTTP/HTTPS, and routing protocols
  • Guidance on Access Control Lists (ACLs)
  • Analysis of network traffic using Wireshark for practical understanding
