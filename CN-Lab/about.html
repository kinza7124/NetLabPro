About NetLabPro

A student-friendly platform to learn and visualize Computer Networks labs with guided explanations.

Lab Features


NetLabPro simplifies complex networking concepts by providing:


  • Step-by-step explanations for each lab experiment
  • Detailed screenshots, diagrams, and pdfs for clarity
  • Hands-on examples for DHCP, DNS, HTTP/HTTPS, and routing protocols
  • Guidance on Access Control Lists (ACLs)
  • Analysis of network traffic using Wireshark for practical understanding

Note: The platform follows the Computer Networks lab curriculum of FAST University, Karachi, covering essential topics while encouraging practical learning.The lab tasks pdfs attached in it might not be perfectly correct, the only sole purpose is to aid learning and this doesnot act as a shortcut to prepare for exams.
